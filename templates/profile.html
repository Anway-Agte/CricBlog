Ranking System

There are 3 ranks in the CricBlog Forum . 

1.) Everyone starts off with a rank of Novice . 
     This just means that your posts won't be seen by other       people as they would of users with a higher rank . 

2.)As your activity increases on our website , you start
     collecting points , every like , reply and view on your             posts contributes towards it , though they should be           from other users . Once your activity breaches the                 minimum required barrier , you become an                             Intermediate . Your new and old posts will both                     be more frequent on the forum lists .  

3.) Building on that , after a lot of posting and                                acknowledgement from fellows , you will be                            promoted to Expert . Your posts would be on the top          of the list and every post will be followed with a                      notification to all other users .
