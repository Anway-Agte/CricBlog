What is CricBlog ? 


An online community made for the fans , by a fan . CricBlog provides you with an exhilarating opportunity to don the expert's hat , and be in the spotlight . 

In the commentary box 

How often have you ranted about or after a game with no one to hear your wisdom ? With CricBlog , the entire community heeds to your opinion and react to it . Provide your insights , and see how they sit with fellow peers . 

The Firing Range

A little banter never hurt anybody , nor will it here . A chance to stick it up to the opposition fanbase before/after games , though keeping the standard high , as our players do . After all , we won't like to be banned . 

Scale up the ranks

The more you engage in conversations and discussions , the more points you get , which in turn better your rank . The better your rank , the more often will people see your posts . Win-win !
